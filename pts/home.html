About Play the Stats

Play the Stats is a statistic based competition that allows users to compete for complete sports packages for only $5. In order to play, you must purchase any number of "shares" of a team in a specific sport. For each "share", you will answer a series of questions about upcoming sporting events. The better your answers, the better your chances are to win prizes. All questions are fact based statistic categories that occur during the sports week.

For more information visit Rules and Regulations.
